The Public Interest
Law Journal of New Zealand

The Public Interest Law Journal of New Zealand is a new annual journal supported by the Law Foundation that will showcase the best written work by New Zealand law students addressing topics of public interest.

The journal is now calling for submissions from law students on articles discussing a variety of topics, such as:

  • Criminal justice
  • Human rights
  • Environmental law
  • Animal law
  • Family law
  • Access to the Legal System

Submissions must be between 2,500 and 12,000 words in length.

Email your submission to piljnz@gmail.com before 7 September 2013.
